# Privacy Policy — FOHT Volunteer App **Last updated:** 8 April 2026 This privacy policy describes how the FOHT Volunteer App ("the app") handles information about its users. The app is provided by **Friends of the Heysen Trail Inc.** ("FOHT", "we", "us") for use by registered volunteers carrying out trail maintenance and related activities on the Heysen Trail in South Australia. The app is intended for use by FOHT volunteers only. It is not directed at children or members of the general public. --- ## 1. What information we collect We collect only the information needed to coordinate volunteer work on the Heysen Trail. Specifically: ### Information you provide to us - **Knack account credentials** (email and password) — used to log in to the FOHT volunteer database. The password is stored on your device only and is never transmitted to us in any form other than the standard Knack login request. - **Phone number** — entered in the app's Settings screen. Used to identify you when uploading work records and to contact you about volunteer activities. - **Photos** — taken or selected from your gallery when you submit a maintenance report or attach a receipt to an expense claim. - **Expense data** — receipt details, amounts, categories, and notes that you enter for reimbursement claims. - **Maintenance reports** — text descriptions and photos of trail issues that you choose to report. ### Information collected automatically - **Location data (GPS)** — collected continuously while you have the app's geofencing feature active, including when the app is in the background. We use this only to: - detect when you cross into and out of the trail-work zone (1 km from the trail) so that work-record times can be generated automatically; - record the GPS coordinates of maintenance issues, water tank readings, and other assets you log; - alert you when you approach a Point of Interest you have asked to be notified about. - **Device information** — operating system version, device model, and app version. This is included in diagnostic uploads (see below) so we can troubleshoot problems. - **Activity logs** — the app records the events it performs (geofence crossings, uploads, errors) in a local log buffer. ### What we do NOT collect - We do not use any advertising networks or tracking SDKs. - We do not collect contacts, calendar entries, browsing history, or any data from other apps on your device. - We do not collect data from sensors other than GPS and the camera. - We do not collect data when you are not using the app, except for GPS location while the geofencing feature is explicitly active. --- ## 2. How we use this information - **Volunteer coordination** — work records, maintenance reports, expense claims, and water tank readings you create are uploaded to the FOHT volunteer database (Knack) and used by FOHT to manage trail maintenance, reimburse volunteers, and prioritise repairs. - **Asset and trail management** — photos and GPS coordinates of issues, campsites, water tanks, and other assets are stored to help FOHT and volunteers locate and maintain them. - **Troubleshooting (beta only)** — during the beta-testing phase, the app can upload a diagnostic snapshot to help us debug problems. The snapshot contains your phone number, recent events, work records, settings (excluding passwords), and the app's console log buffer. Diagnostic uploads happen either when you tap the **Send Diagnostics** button in Settings, or automatically after a successful work-record upload. You can stop diagnostic uploads from happening automatically by avoiding work-record uploads or by asking us to remove your account from the beta. - **No marketing** — we do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose. --- ## 3. Where information is stored Your information is stored in the following systems, all operated by us or by service providers we have engaged: | Where | What | Operator | |---|---|---| | Your device (encrypted) | Login credentials, session tokens, cached records | Local storage on your device only | | Knack | Volunteer profile, work records, activities, maintenance issues, expense claims, water tank data | Knack Inc., USA | | Microsoft Azure Blob Storage | Photos (maintenance reports, expense receipts) and diagnostic snapshots | Microsoft Corporation, hosted in Australia | | Microsoft Azure Document Intelligence | Receipt photos sent for OCR text extraction | Microsoft Corporation, hosted in Australia | | Myriota | Water tank sensor telemetry data (no personal data) | Myriota Pty Ltd, Australia | We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to anyone. We share it only with the service providers listed above, and only to the extent necessary to operate the app. --- ## 4. How long we keep it - **Work records, maintenance reports, expense claims, photos**: retained indefinitely in the FOHT Knack database as part of the historical record of volunteer activity on the trail. - **GPS event logs (on your device)**: removed automatically when you start a new activity in the app, or when you uninstall the app. - **Diagnostic snapshots**: retained for 30 days in Azure Blob Storage and then automatically deleted. - **Account credentials (on your device)**: retained until you log out or uninstall the app. --- ## 5. Your rights and choices - **Access and correction**: You can view and correct your volunteer profile data through the FOHT Knack portal or by contacting us. - **Deletion**: You can ask us to delete your account and the data associated with it by contacting us at the address below. Note that historical work records may be retained in anonymised form for FOHT's records. - **Permissions on your device**: You can revoke the app's access to location, camera, and notifications at any time through your device's Settings app. The app's geofencing and reporting features will not work without these permissions. - **Stop using the app**: You can uninstall the app at any time. This removes all data stored locally on your device. --- ## 6. Security - Login credentials and authentication tokens are stored on your device using encrypted secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain via `expo-secure-store`). - All communication between the app and our service providers (Knack, Azure, Myriota) is encrypted in transit using HTTPS / TLS. - Photos and diagnostic snapshots are uploaded directly to Azure Blob Storage using time-limited signed URLs (SAS tokens). - No personal data is included in URLs, query strings, or unencrypted channels. We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no system is perfectly secure. If you become aware of a security issue with the app, please contact us immediately at the address below. --- ## 7. Children The app is not intended for use by anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us and we will remove their data. --- ## 8. Changes to this policy We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent change. For substantive changes, we will notify users through the app or by email before the change takes effect. --- ## 9.
